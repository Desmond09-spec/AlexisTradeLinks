About AlexisTradeLinks

Our Journey, Our Family, Our Commitment

AlexisTradeLinks is a proud Nigerian brand, led by Aleriwa Sunkanmi. Aleriwa is happily married to Janet Sunkanmi and is a devoted father to Alexander and Nathaniel. He founded this company based on trust and excellence. We specialize in computer sales and servicing, as well as mobile and web app development.

Our expertise also includes reliable residential and business inverter solutions, along with professional CCTV installations for improved security. We are dedicated to providing quality products and services while building strong relationships within our community.
